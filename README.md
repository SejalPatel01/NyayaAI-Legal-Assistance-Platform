# ⚖️ NyayaAI Guardian

> AI-powered Legal Assessment & Case Preparation Platform

NyayaAI helps ordinary citizens understand legal disputes, organize evidence, assess case strength, generate legal documents, and receive structured legal guidance through a multi-agent AI architecture.

---

## 🚀 Why NyayaAI?

Many people:

* Don't know their legal rights
* Don't know which documents are important
* Cannot afford immediate legal consultation
* Struggle to organize evidence

NyayaAI acts as an intelligent legal assistant that guides users step-by-step through their case.

---

# ✨ Core Features

### 🧠 Multi-Agent Legal Intelligence

NyayaAI uses a specialized multi-agent architecture:

* Issue Classifier Agent
* Legal Advisor Agent
* Document Analyzer Agent
* Action Generator Agent

Each agent performs a dedicated task and contributes to the final legal assessment.

---

### 📄 Document Intelligence

Upload:

* PDF
* Images
* Screenshots

NyayaAI automatically:

* Extracts text
* Simplifies legal language
* Identifies clauses
* Extracts obligations
* Detects deadlines
* Highlights risks

---

### ✅ Evidence Verification Engine

Automatically verifies legal evidence such as:

* Rent Agreements
* Bank Statements
* Bank Receipts
* WhatsApp Screenshots
* Emails
* SMS Records
* Property Handover Documents
* Witness Statements

Strict document-type-driven verification prevents false positives.

---

### 📊 Case Strength Analysis

Generates:

* Case Strength Score
* Risk Rating
* Evidence Gap Analysis
* Missing Document Detection

---

### 🗂 Evidence Gap Advisor

Shows:

* Missing critical evidence
* Why it matters
* Recommended next upload
* Expected score improvement

---

### 📅 Legal Timeline Generator

Automatically creates a chronological timeline of events based on:

* Uploaded documents
* User statements
* Extracted dates

---

### 📜 Legal Document Generation

Generate:

* Legal Notices
* Consumer Complaints
* Demand Letters
* Case Preparation Documents
